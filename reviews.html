Dream house by the sea!

We were welcomed very warmly by Viktor, owners son, who invited us to Villa Strmo.

Only a few kilometers from Trogir, you can reach this beautiful, completely newly

renovated Villa via a narrow steep road. Viktor took a lot of time and showed us

everything in and around the house. For example, the service from the great jacuzzi

which we enjoyed every day.

The villa actually exceeded our expectations! The photos in advance were really nice,

but this view from the garden to the sea was overwhelming. Garden door up

and into the crystal clear sea. I A metal guide makes it very easy to get into the water.

Only a few meters away you can reach a beach, which is very suitable for small

children.

Villa has 5 very beautiful rooms each with its own bathroom. The house is very

lovingly furnished and has a special flair with a feel good factor. In the garden there is

the outdoor kitchen, equipped with everything you can imagine (induction cooker,

oven, dishwasher, large refrigerators, dishes, glasses...). The large garden has many

different places where you can sit together, but also be undisturbed. Sun beds,

porch swing, different lovely lounges. We all felt completely comfortable this week

and will definitely be back.

Again, our warmest thanks to Viktor, Goran and Lea for the very warm hospitality.

We look forward to a reunion.
